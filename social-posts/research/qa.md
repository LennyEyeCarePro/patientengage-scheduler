# Step 6: Pre-Gen Q&A — Social Stream

## Confirmed Answers

### 1. What is the primary angle for this page?
**Answer:** "Zero-Effort Revenue Recovery" Pivot — Problem-first (Abandoned Social Media) + Zero-Effort Automation.
- Emotional hook: The "Abandoned Storefront" (Invisibility Fear) — "Your digital storefront looks closed even when you're open."
- Position Social Stream as the "automated heartbeat" of the practice
- Not about viral fame — about **Clinical Presence**
- For Dr. Someday and Sarah, the problem isn't wanting to post; it's sustaining it

### 2. What is the killer stat?
**Answer:** "214,509 Automated Interactions" + "The 1% Specialty Found Revenue" (The "Surround Sound" Effect)
- Use "214k" for network scale proof
- Pair with the Capture Gap quantifier: "If an automated campaign reaches your current patients and just 1% book a specialty eval, that's $15,000 in found revenue for a $49 investment."
- Raw volume is impressive but abstract — the Growth Chaser needs the revenue math

### 3. Who is the target persona?
**Answer:** Same 3-persona framework, confirmed:
1. Dr. "Someday" — can't sustain social posting
2. Sarah "The Swiss Army Knife" — can't sustain it either
3. The Growth Chaser — wants the Capture Gap closed

### 4. Feature name for the marketing page?
**Answer:** **Social Stream**
- "Social Posts" sounds like a chore Sarah has to do
- "Social Stream" sounds like a utility — like water or electricity — that just flows into the practice
- Implies multi-channel nature (FB, IG, GBP) without sounding like a manual task

### 5. Connection opportunity angle?
**Answer:** Include as a "Risk Audit" — highlight it aggressively
- Copy angle: "80% of practices are 'ghosting' their Instagram audience. Are you one of them?"
- "Even if your profile is connected, if it hasn't been updated in 30 days, you are paying an Invisibility Tax."
- Frame as risk/loss, not opportunity/growth

### 6. Case study approach?
**Answer:** Hybrid — Network-level stats + One anonymized practice spotlight
- Network stats prove the "Pill" works at scale
- Practice spotlight: "A 3-doctor clinic that reclaimed 12 hours of staff time while seeing a 14% lift in second-pair inquiries"
- Numbers convince the brain, stories convince the heart

### 7. CTA?
**Answer:** Standard Book a Demo (implied — no pushback)

### 7. Personas: Social-Specific Refinements
**Answer:** Keep the 3 Campaigns personas, sharpened for "Social Malignancy":
1. **Dr. Someday** → "Abandoned Building" syndrome — shame that their social looks dead
2. **Sarah** → "Canva Fatigue" — resenting the hours wasted on posts with zero reach
3. **The Growth Chaser** → "Google Ghost" — missing local SEO because they don't post to GBP

### 8. Unique angle: GBP Stealth SEO
**Answer:** Most competitors and owners think social is just Facebook/Instagram. They ignore GBP Posts.
- Posting to GBP is a "hidden" SEO signal
- Every Social Stream post to Google Business Profile signals to Google that the practice is active
- This directly influences their "Map Pack" ranking
- Turns Social from "Fluff" into "Search Engine Dominance"

### 9. Additional framing concepts provided:
- **"Invisibility Tax"** — the cost of inactive social channels
- **"Automated Heartbeat"** — Social Stream as the vital sign of the practice
- **"Clinical Presence"** — not viral fame, professional visibility
- **"Surround Sound Effect"** — multi-channel ambient brand presence
