# Overview: Social Posts (Social Stream)

## What It Is
Social Posts is PatientEngage's automated social media content engine. It publishes professionally designed, eye care-specific content to a practice's Facebook page, Instagram business account, and Google Business Profile — on a weekly schedule, with zero effort from the practice. Practices subscribe to curated content streams and receive posts automatically. The content team creates one post; it publishes to 731+ locations simultaneously.

## The Problem It Solves
Social media is every practice's guilt trip. They know they should be posting — to Facebook, to Instagram, to Google Business. But between exams, insurance paperwork, and staffing, "update social media" slides to the bottom of the list. The Facebook page hasn't been touched since 2023. Instagram was set up but never posted to. Google Business Profile shows no recent updates. Meanwhile, the practice across town has a lively feed with seasonal tips, eye health education, and professional imagery. That practice looks modern and active. Yours looks abandoned. Patients notice.

## How It Works
1. **Connect your social accounts** — link your Facebook page, Instagram business account, and Google Business Profile to PatientEngage (one-time setup)
2. **Streams subscribe automatically** — every practice gets the main EyeCarePro content stream (4 posts/month). Practices offering specialty services (Dry Eye, Vision Therapy, Scleral Lenses, Sports Vision) can subscribe to specialty streams for additional targeted content.
3. **Content publishes on schedule** — posts go live on their scheduled dates and times (typically Wednesdays at 9 am EST for the main stream) to all connected channels simultaneously
4. **Track results in the calendar** — the practice sees all Social Stream posts AND Campaign social posts on a unified calendar with Facebook Insights and Instagram Insights in the sidebar

### Important Distinctions
- **Not DIY** — practices don't write, design, or schedule posts. Everything is pre-created and auto-published.
- **Not campaign-driven** — Social Stream runs independently of One-Click Campaigns. Campaigns generate their own social posts. Social Stream is the always-on background content engine.
- **Not just Facebook** — posts go to Facebook, Instagram, AND Google Business Profile simultaneously, with platform-optimized images (landscape for Facebook/Google, square for Instagram).
- **Not generic** — content is created specifically for eye care: seasonal awareness months, clinical education, vision health tips. Not repurposed dental or general health content.

- Office managers told to "post more on social media" who don't know what to post

- Practices offering specialty services (Dry Eye, Vision Therapy, Scleral Lenses) who want targeted social content
- Practices already using EyeCarePro for websites/SEO who want to extend their digital presence to social channels

## The Value It Adds
- Professional social media presence without hiring a social media manager or agency
- Consistent weekly content that keeps the practice visible and active on all channels
- Seasonal and topical content timed to eye care awareness months and patient relevance
- Platform-optimized formatting (separate images for Facebook vs Instagram)
- Google Business Profile posts that signal activity to Google's local search algorithm

## Best Use Case
Use Social Posts when you want a professional, consistent social media presence across Facebook, Instagram, and Google Business Profile without the time, content, or expertise to do it yourself. For patient-level outreach (emails, landing pages, multi-channel campaigns), pair with One-Click Campaigns. For recall and reminder automation, pair with Recalls & Reminders.
