# Page Brief: Social Posts Marketing Page

## Page Goal
Convince optometry practice owners and office managers that Social Posts eliminates the #1 social media barrier — having nothing to post — by delivering professional, eye care-specific content to their Facebook, Instagram, and Google Business Profile automatically, every week, with zero effort after connecting their accounts.

## Target Audience
- Practice owners (ODs) whose Facebook page hasn't been updated in months
- Office managers told to "post something to social media" who don't know what to post
- Multi-location groups that need consistent social branding across all offices without hiring per-location social media coordinators
- Practices who tried Hootsuite or Buffer, posted a few times, and gave up (blank canvas problem)
- Practices already using EyeCarePro who haven't connected their social accounts yet

## Key Messages (Priority Order)
1. **Your social media runs itself** — professional content publishes to 3 channels automatically every week
2. **Eye care content, not generic filler** — seasonal, topical, clinically relevant posts designed for optometry
3. **Three channels, zero effort** — Facebook + Instagram + Google Business Profile with platform-optimized images
4. **Specialty content for specialty services** — subscribe to Dry Eye, Vision Therapy, or Scleral Lens streams for targeted content
5. **Network-proven scale** — 214,509 interactions across 2,696 locations in a single month
6. **Connect once, content flows forever** — no monthly content calendar, no weekly posting stress
