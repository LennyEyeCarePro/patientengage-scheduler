# Case Study: Social Posts — Network-Level + Stream Spotlight

## Why a Network Case Study (Not Per-Practice)

Social Posts is fundamentally different from Campaigns. Campaigns drives direct appointments with measurable per-practice ROI (email → open → click → appointment). Social Posts creates **ambient brand presence** — consistent, professional content that keeps the practice visible on social channels.

There's no "80 appointments from one social post" story. Instead, the story is about scale, consistency, and the multiplier effect of one content team serving 731+ practices simultaneously. The case study is the network itself.

---

## Network Case Study: "One Team, 731 Practices, 214,509 Interactions"

### The Challenge
Independent optometry practices know they should be active on social media. But between patients, insurance paperwork, and staffing, "update Facebook" never makes it off the to-do list. The result: abandoned Facebook pages, empty Instagram feeds, and Google Business Profile listings with no recent updates. The practice across town with an active social presence looks more professional, more modern, and more trustworthy — even if the clinical care is identical.

Hiring a social media manager costs $40,000–$60,000/year. Paying an agency runs $500–$1,500/month. Creating content yourself takes 5–10 hours/month that the office manager doesn't have. For most independent practices, the math doesn't work.

### The Solution
Social Stream provides professional, curated social content to 2,696 locations simultaneously. One content team creates 22 posts per month across 9 specialized streams. Each post automatically publishes to every subscribed practice's Facebook, Instagram, and Google Business Profile — with platform-optimized images (landscape for Facebook/Google, square for Instagram).

The practice connects their accounts once. After that, content flows automatically, every week, forever.

### Key Performance Highlights
- **214,509 interactions in a single month** — across all practices, all channels, all posts combined. This is the collective social footprint of the network.
- **22 posts served 2,696 locations** — one post created at the admin level reaches hundreds of practices automatically. The main EyeCarePro stream reaches 731 locations per post.
- **2.6 posts per location per month** — without the practice writing, designing, or scheduling anything. For practices subscribed to specialty streams, the number is higher (10+ posts/month).
- **174,401 Facebook impressions** — the primary channel for social reach. Patients see practice posts in their Facebook feeds.
- **39,708 Instagram impressions** — growing channel, but only 20% of practices have Instagram connected (massive upside).

2. **Save Your Vision Month** (Mar 11) — timed to national awareness month
3. **New Season New Glasses** (Mar 18) — promotional/lifestyle

### Performance Highlights

- **81 shares** — patients sharing eye care content from their doctor's page. This extends reach beyond followers to their friends and family.

## Specialty Stream Spotlight: Dry Eye

### Profile
- **Stream type:** Clinical specialty (Dry Eye awareness and education)
- **Post frequency:** 2 posts/month
- **Locations subscribed:** 102

### March 2026 Performance

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Posts | 2 |
| Facebook Impressions | 2,970 |
| Instagram Impressions | 780 |
| Google Clicks | 27 |
| Total Interactions | 3,831 |
| Per-Location-Per-Post | 18.8 |

### Why This Matters
The Dry Eye stream's per-location-per-post performance (18.8) is **nearly identical to the main stream (18.2)** — despite being a niche clinical topic with 7× fewer locations. This proves specialty content isn't filler; it resonates with the audiences that need it.

A practice subscribed to the main stream + Dry Eye stream gets 6 posts/month (4 + 2) — all relevant, all automated, all professionally designed.

## The Connection Opportunity

### Current State
| Channel | Connected | Percentage |

| Google Business | 2,120 | 73% |
| Facebook | 1,308 | 45% |
| Instagram | 575 | 20% |

### The Untapped Potential
- **1,340 practices have no Facebook** — Social Stream content has nowhere to publish on Facebook for these practices
- **2,329 practices have no Instagram** — 80% of the network is missing Instagram entirely
- **If all practices connected Instagram**, the 39,708 Instagram impressions could grow to ~198,540 (4.7× increase)
- **226 broken connections** need repair (101 Google + 125 Facebook)

### The Story
214,509 interactions happened with only 45% Facebook adoption and 20% Instagram adoption. The content is ready. The infrastructure works. The practices just need to connect.
