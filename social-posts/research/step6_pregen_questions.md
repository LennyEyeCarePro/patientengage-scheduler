## Questions for Lenny

### Q1: What is the primary angle for this page?

Based on Steps 1–5, I propose three angle options:

**Option A: "The Social Media You Never Have to Touch"** (Zero-effort automation)
Lead with the relief of never having to open Facebook, Instagram, or think about what to post. The practice connects their accounts once and professional content publishes every week forever.

**Option B: "Your Practice Is Invisible on Social Media"** (Problem-first / wake-up call)
Lead with the painful truth: the practice's Facebook hasn't been updated in months, Instagram doesn't exist, and GBP shows "no recent updates." Social Stream fixes all three — automatically, with eye care-specific content.

**Option C: "Professional Content for Every Channel, Every Week"** (Content quality + consistency)
Lead with what patients actually see: seasonal, relevant, professionally designed posts appearing on the practice's Facebook, Instagram, and Google Business Profile. Emphasize the quality of the curated content (not just that it's automated).

**My recommendation:** A + B combo — lead with the problem (invisible/abandoned social media), solve with zero-effort automation. The content quality is the proof point, not the lead angle.
