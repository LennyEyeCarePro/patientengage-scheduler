# Video Script: Social Posts (Social Stream)

## Format
- **Length:** 60–90 seconds
- **Style:** Screen recording walkthrough with voiceover
- **Audience:** Practice owners and office managers considering PatientEngage

---

## Script

### Opening (0:00–0:10)
**[Show: Practice's Facebook page — last post dated 8 months ago. Instagram profile — 2 posts total. Google Business Profile — "No recent updates."]**

**Voiceover:** "When was the last time your practice posted to social media? If you can't remember — neither can your patients."

### Problem (0:10–0:20)
**[Show: Office manager opening Canva, staring at blank canvas, closing the tab. Quick montage: empty Facebook page, dormant Instagram, Google listing with no posts.]**

**Voiceover:** "You know you should be posting. But between patients, insurance, and staffing — 'update social media' never makes it off the to-do list. So your Facebook goes dark. Instagram stays empty. And your Google listing says nothing."

### Solution Intro (0:20–0:35)
**[Show: PatientEngage Social Stream calendar view — posts appearing on the calendar automatically. Zoom into a post: "Seasonal Allergies" with professional imagery.]**

**Voiceover:** "Social Stream changes that. Connect your Facebook, Instagram, and Google Business Profile once — and professional, eye care content starts publishing automatically. Every week. You don't write it, design it, or schedule it."

### How It Works (0:35–0:50)
**[Show: Calendar filling up with posts — teal-bordered Social Stream posts appearing on Wednesdays, additional specialty stream posts on other days. Toggle the filter to show Campaign Posts alongside.]**

**Voiceover:** "Your content team creates posts designed specifically for eye care — Seasonal Allergies in spring, UV Protection in summer, Save Your Vision Month when it matters most. Each post goes to all three channels with images optimized for each platform."

### Scale (0:50–1:05)
**[Show: Admin Social Reports dashboard — 214,509 total interactions, 2,696 locations, 7,098 posts. Zoom into stream breakdown showing 731 locations on the main stream.]**

**Voiceover:** "Across nearly 2,700 locations, Social Stream published over 7,000 posts last month — generating 214,000 interactions. One post from our team reaches 731 practices simultaneously. That's the power of curated content at scale."

### Specialty Streams (1:05–1:15)
**[Show: Stream dropdown showing specialty options — Dry Eye, Vision Therapy, Scleral Lenses, Sports Vision. Calendar updates to show specialty posts interspersed with main stream posts.]**

**Voiceover:** "Offer Dry Eye services? Subscribe to the Dry Eye stream. Do Vision Therapy? Add that stream. Your social content automatically matches your clinical specialties — targeted content for your specific patient base."

### Close (1:15–1:25)
**[Show: Practice's social calendar — full month of posts, all automated. PatientEngage logo + CTA.]**

**Voiceover:** "Social Stream. Professional eye care content on every channel — without you lifting a finger. Connect your accounts and let the content flow. Talk to your EyeCarePro team to get started."

---

## Key Frames for Thumbnail
1. Split screen: abandoned Facebook page (left) vs. active, content-rich Facebook page (right)
2. Calendar view packed with colorful post thumbnails
3. "214,509 Interactions" stat overlay on social media icons

## Production Notes
- All screen recordings from PatientEngage practice view (NOT SeaShore House)
- Emphasize the calendar filling itself — this is the visual metaphor for "zero effort"
- The Facebook/Instagram/Google trifecta should be visually represented throughout
- Use North Park Vision or similar practice with multiple specialty streams subscribed for the calendar density shots
