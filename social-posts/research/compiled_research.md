## PRODUCT FACTS (Steps 1 + 4)

### What Social Stream Does:
1. **Facebook Posts** — Landscape-format image + ~44 words of professional copy, published to practice's Facebook page
2. **Instagram Posts** — Square-format image (separate upload, platform-optimized) + caption, published to practice's Instagram
3. **Google Business Profile Posts** — Image + CTA link, published to practice's GBP listing (visible in Google Maps/Search)

All three channels publish simultaneously from a single admin-created post.

### How It Works:
1. Practice connects Facebook, Instagram, and/or Google Business Profile to PatientEngage (one-time)
2. Practice is subscribed to content streams (main + optional specialty streams)
3. Content team creates posts at admin level with platform-optimized images
4. Posts auto-publish to all subscribed locations' connected channels on schedule
5. Practice sees all posts on a unified calendar with engagement insights

## PATIENT EXPERIENCE (Step 4)

The patient journey through Social Posts:
1. Scrolls Facebook → sees practice post about Seasonal Allergies with professional imagery
2. Searches for practice on Google → sees recent GBP posts in "Updates" section
3. Browses Instagram → sees practice's UV Protection post with square-format imagery
4. Social presence creates ambient brand awareness — practice stays top-of-mind between visits

**Key distinction from Campaigns:** Social Posts creates ambient brand presence, not direct conversion. There is no email-to-appointment tracking. The value is visibility, professionalism, and consistency — not direct ROI.
