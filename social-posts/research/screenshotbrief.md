## Shot 1 (HERO): `ss_social_01_practice_calendar.png`
**What:** A practice's Social page showing a full month of automated posts — the "calendar that filled itself."

**Priority:** HIGHEST — this is the hero image on the page.

**Setup:**
1. Navigate to: `/u/{practiceId}/marketing/social/{locationId}`
2. Pick a practice subscribed to **EyeCarePro main stream + at least one specialty stream** (this matches the case study architecture of 4 main + 2 specialty posts/month = 6+ posts)
3. Set month to **March 2026** (matches all case study data)
4. Check ALL filter boxes: Social Stream Posts + Campaign Posts (FB) + Campaign Posts (IG) + Campaign Posts (GMB)
5. Capture the full calendar with 12–15 posts visible, thumbnails, titles, and times

**Crop:** Calendar grid with sidebar showing Facebook Insights + Instagram Insights. Include the filter checkboxes so viewers see how posts are categorized.

**Frame:** Browser chrome (red/yellow/green dots)

**Critical rules:**
- **MUST NOT be SeaShore House (#1718)**
- Practice name should be blurred/anonymized OR use a practice that has given explicit permission
- Show density — every few days should have a post
- Color-coded borders should distinguish Social Stream vs Campaign posts

## General Screenshot Rules

- **Dates:** All screenshots use **March 2026** data (matches case study + page numbers exactly)
- **File location:** Save to `/social-posts/screenshots/` with filenames as specified above
- **Browser chrome frame** on every shot (red/yellow/green dots)
- **Size:** ~1400px wide for crisp rendering at 700px displayed width
- **NEVER include SeaShore House Optical (#1718)** in any screenshot or data
- **Anonymize practice names** unless explicit written permission exists
- **Real captures only** — no mockups, no generated images
- **Consistency check:** Every number visible in a screenshot must match the numbers quoted on the page. If a number doesn't match, the screenshot gets retaken — not the page copy
