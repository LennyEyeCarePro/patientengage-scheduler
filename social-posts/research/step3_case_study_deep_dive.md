## Why Social Posts Tells a Network Story, Not a Practice Story

Unlike Campaigns (where individual practices show dramatic funnel metrics like 80+ appointments), the Social Posts value proposition is about **automated, consistent, zero-effort content delivery at scale**. No single practice's Facebook impressions are dramatic on their own — but 214,509 total interactions across 2,696 locations from 22 admin-created posts tells a powerful story.

The case study structure here is:
1. **Network-level** — the scale and automation story
2. **Stream-level** — how one post reaches 731 practices simultaneously
3. **Practice-level** — what a typical practice sees on their calendar without lifting a finger

The main EyeCarePro stream is the flagship content channel. It serves the broadest audience — general eye care awareness content timed to seasonal themes.

### Key Calculations [CALCULATED]
- **Per-post reach:** 53,131 ÷ 4 = ~13,283 interactions per post
- **Per-location-per-post:** 53,131 ÷ (4 × 731) = ~18.2 interactions per post per location
- **Facebook engagement rate:** (640 likes + 16 comments + 81 shares) ÷ 40,818 impressions = 1.8% engagement rate
- **Share of all stream interactions:** 53,131 ÷ 78,911 = 67% — the main stream drives two-thirds of all Social Stream engagement

## Connection Opportunity Analysis [OBSERVED + CALCULATED]

The biggest case study insight isn't about engagement — it's about **untapped potential**. From Step 2 Social Connections data:

| Channel | Connected | Not Connected | Broken |
|---|---|---|---|
| Google Business Profile | 2,120 (73%) | 535 (18%) | 101 (3.5%) |
| Facebook | 1,308 (45%) | 1,340 (46%) | 125 (4.3%) |
| Instagram | 575 (20%) | 2,329 (80%) | 0 |

### The Untapped Reach [CALCULATED]
- **1,340 practices have no Facebook connected** — they're subscribed to Social Stream but posts have nowhere to go on FB
- **2,329 practices have no Instagram connected** — 80% of the network is missing Instagram entirely
- **226 broken connections** (101 Google + 125 Facebook) — practices that WERE connected but lost their link
- **If all practices connected Facebook:** 1,308 → 2,696 locations would double the Facebook reach
- **If all practices connected Instagram:** 575 → 2,696 locations would 4.7x the Instagram reach

### What This Means for the Marketing Page
The Social Posts page should tell two stories:
1. **"Here's what 214,509 interactions looks like — and that's with only 45% of practices on Facebook and 20% on Instagram"** — implies massive upside
2. **"Connect your accounts and the content is already waiting"** — the Social Stream has been publishing to practices that haven't connected. Once they connect, the content starts flowing immediately.
