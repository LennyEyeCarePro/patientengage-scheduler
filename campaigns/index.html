Common questions

Things practice owners ask before launching their first campaign.

Every question below is one we hear regularly. Here is the direct answer.

What kinds of campaigns are available? ↓
Hundreds of eye care-specific campaigns covering seasonal promotions (Spring Allergies, UV Protection), awareness months (Save Vision Month, Women's Eye Health Month), clinical education (Dry Eye tips, Diabetic Eyecare), brand partnerships (Marchon, VSP, Alcon co-branded content), and promotional campaigns (Tax Season New Eyewear). New campaigns are added regularly to match the industry calendar.
Do I have to design or write anything? ↓
No. Every campaign comes with professionally designed email templates, social media posts, images, and a dedicated landing page. You do not write copy, design graphics, or create landing pages. You pick the campaign, pick the month, and click schedule. Everything is pre-built and ready to go.
How does the multi-channel execution work? ↓
When a campaign fires, it pushes content across up to five channels simultaneously: an email blast to your patient list, Facebook posts, Instagram posts, a Google Business Profile update, and a dedicated campaign landing page. Each channel gets content matched to the campaign theme. You connect your social accounts once, and campaigns publish automatically from that point forward.
Can I assign different campaigns to different locations? ↓
Yes. Multi-location practices can assign different campaigns to different offices. Each location gets its own email timeline, its own social posts, and its own performance tracking. One office can run Spring Allergies while another runs Save Vision Month. Or you can run the same campaign across all locations for consistent branding. It is entirely flexible.
How do you track if campaigns actually drive appointments? ↓
The dashboard tracks EHR Email Appointments, which are appointments booked within 7 days of a campaign email event (an open or a click). This gives you direct attribution from campaign send to booked exam. Across the network, 34,623 appointments were attributed to campaigns in March 2026 alone. It is real ROI measurement, not guesswork.
"I already use Mailchimp or Constant Contact." ↓
How many campaigns did you actually send last quarter? If the answer is "a few" or "I meant to," that is the blank canvas problem. Mailchimp gives you an editor and says "go." Campaigns-To-Go gives you a finished campaign with email, social posts, and a landing page, all ready to launch. No writing, no designing, no posting to five different platforms. One click replaces an entire month of DIY marketing effort.
"We already have Solutionreach or Demandforce." ↓
Solutionreach and Demandforce are excellent for automated reminders and recalls. But when was the last time they ran a multi-channel Spring Allergies campaign with matching Facebook posts, Instagram content, GBP updates, and a landing page? We are not replacing your recall system. We are filling the marketing gap that automated tools do not touch. Different job, different tool.
How much does Campaigns-To-Go cost? ↓
Campaigns-To-Go is included in EyeCarePro marketing programs. The equivalent multi-channel marketing executed by an agency costs $2,000 to $5,000 per month. Competitors like Solutionreach and Demandforce charge $300 to $500 per month for email-only campaigns with generic templates. We deliver eye care-specific, five-channel campaigns as part of your marketing program.
