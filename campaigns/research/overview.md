# Overview: Campaigns-To-Go

## What It Is
Campaigns-To-Go is a multi-channel marketing campaign platform built into PatientEngage that lets optometry practices launch professionally designed email, social media, and landing page campaigns with a single click. Practices choose from a library of hundreds of pre-built, eye care-specific campaigns — seasonal promotions, awareness months, brand partnerships, clinical education — and schedule them to run automatically across Email, Facebook, Instagram, Google Business Profile, and dedicated landing pages.

## The Problem It Solves
Marketing is the practice owner's least favorite job. They know they should be sending emails, posting to social media, and running seasonal promotions — but they don't have the time, the design skills, or the strategy to execute consistently. So they do nothing. Or they pay a marketing agency $2,000–$5,000/month. Or they post sporadically to Facebook and hope for the best. Meanwhile, their patient base lapses because nobody reminded them that it's time for an eye exam, that allergy season is here, or that the practice carries the frames they've been looking for.

## How It Works
1. **Browse the campaign library** — hundreds of pre-built campaigns organized by topic: Dry Eye, Allergies, UV Protection, Save Vision Month, Women's Eye Health, Tax Season Eyewear, Diabetic Eyecare, brand partnerships (Marchon, VSP), and more
2. **Schedule with one click** — pick a month, assign the campaign to one or multiple locations, and it goes live automatically
3. **Multi-channel execution** — each campaign pushes content across up to 5 channels: Email to patient lists, Facebook posts, Instagram posts, Google Business Profile updates, and a dedicated landing page
4. **Track performance in real time** — the campaign dashboard shows Emails Sent, Unique Opens, Email Clicks, Social Impressions, Landing Page Views, and Appointments booked within 7 days of email events
5. **Admin oversight** — EyeCarePro's team monitors all 647 practices from the Campaigns Manager, handling scheduling, approvals, and troubleshooting

### Important Distinctions
- **Not DIY** — practices don't design campaigns, write copy, or create landing pages. Everything is pre-built and professionally designed.
- **Not just email** — campaigns span Email + Facebook + Instagram + GBP + Landing Pages. It's a coordinated multi-channel push.
- **Not self-service scheduling for all practices** — some practices are managed by EyeCarePro's team (ECP Scheduler), while others have external scheduling links or VSP Scheduler access.
- **Not a generic email platform** — every campaign is designed specifically for eye care. There is no Mailchimp-style "build your own" editor.

## Best Use Case
Use Campaigns-To-Go when you want professionally designed, multi-channel marketing campaigns without the time, cost, or expertise required to do it yourself. For patient-level outreach (recalls, reminders, confirmations), pair with Recalls & Reminders. For real-time communication, pair with 2-Way Texting or the Real Time Scheduler.
