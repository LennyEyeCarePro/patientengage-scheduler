# Competitive Research: Campaigns-To-Go

## Competitive Positioning
Campaigns-To-Go is NOT competing with Mailchimp or Constant Contact on email features. It competes on **done-for-you simplicity, eye care specificity, and multi-channel coordination**. The product occupies the "Managed Multi-Channel" niche — pre-built campaigns, one-click scheduling, 5-channel execution, and admin oversight. Practices don't build campaigns; they choose and schedule them.

## Industry Context
- 60%+ of small medical practices do NOT actively market beyond their website and word-of-mouth
- The average optometry practice spends $500–$2,000/month on marketing — most of it on SEO and directory listings, not active campaigns
- Email open rates in healthcare average 21–25% (higher than retail at 15–17%) — patients actually read health-related emails
- Social media posting consistency is the #1 challenge for small practices — they start strong and fade within weeks
- Multi-channel marketing (email + social + landing pages) increases campaign effectiveness by 3–5x vs. single-channel — but fewer than 10% of independent practices execute multi-channel
- The "content calendar" is the bane of the office manager's existence — planning, creating, scheduling, and tracking content across platforms is a full-time job

## Top 3 Direct Competitors

### 1. Solutionreach — The "Legacy Engagement" Rival
**Most established competitor.** Deep EHR integrations, automated campaigns, patient surveys, and recall sequences.
- **How they compete:** Market as an "all-in-one patient engagement platform" with automated email campaigns tied to EHR data (birthday campaigns, recall reminders, reactivation)
- **Their Edge:** EHR-triggered campaigns fire automatically based on patient data — no manual scheduling needed. Deep integrations with most optometry PMS/EHR systems.
- **Pricing:** $300–$500/month per location
- **Our Counter:** Solutionreach campaigns are **template-based, not content-rich.** They fire automated triggers (birthday, recall) but don't provide the seasonal, educational, brand-partnership marketing content that fills a practice's social feed and inbox. We're not competing on automation — we're providing the **editorial calendar they can't build themselves.**

### 2. Demandforce (Internet Brands) — The "Bundled Platform" Rival
Owned by Internet Brands (same parent as WebMD). Focuses on reputation management + email marketing for healthcare.
- **How they compete:** Bundle email campaigns with review generation, online scheduling, and SEO. Market heavily to dental and optometry practices.
- **Their Edge:** Strong review generation and patient reactivation features. Good integration with Practice Fusion and other EHRs.
- **Pricing:** $300–$400/month per location
- **Our Counter:** Demandforce campaigns are **generic health templates**, not eye care-specific. No Marchon frame campaign, no VSP Sports Eye Safety campaign, no "Tax Season New Eyewear" promo. We provide **industry-specific content** created by people who know optometry — not repurposed dental templates with the word "dentist" swapped for "doctor."

### 3. Constant Contact / Mailchimp — The "DIY Email" Rival
Not healthcare-specific, but the most common "we do our own email" tool practices use.
- **How they compete:** Low cost ($20–$80/month), drag-and-drop email builder, basic automation
- **Their Edge:** Cheap. Familiar. Easy to start.
- **Our Counter:** **The blank canvas problem.** Mailchimp gives you an editor and says "go." The practice still has to write the subject line, design the email, create the images, build the landing page, and post to social media separately. 90% of practices that sign up for Mailchimp send 2–3 emails and stop. Campaigns-To-Go eliminates the blank canvas entirely — everything is pre-built, pre-designed, and pre-scheduled.

## The "Multi-Channel Gap" — Where Everyone Else Falls Short

### The Problem with Email-Only Competitors
Solutionreach, Demandforce, Weave, and Mailchimp are all primarily **email tools.** They may offer social posting as an afterthought, but none provide:
- Coordinated multi-channel campaigns (email + FB + IG + GBP + landing page)
- Pre-built social content matched to the email campaign theme
- Landing pages that tie the campaign together

### The Campaigns-To-Go Advantage
A single campaign pushes to **5 channels simultaneously:**
1. **Email** — to the practice's patient list (EHR-sourced or uploaded)
2. **Facebook** — branded posts matching the campaign theme
3. **Instagram** — visual posts for the practice's feed
4. **Google Business Profile** — GBP posts that boost local search
5. **Landing Page** — dedicated page for the campaign topic, driving page views and SEO

This is the equivalent of what a marketing agency would charge $2,000–$5,000/month to produce manually.

## Objection Handling

### 1. "I already use Mailchimp / Constant Contact"
"Great — how many campaigns did you send last quarter? If the answer is 'a few' or 'I meant to,' that's the problem. Mailchimp gives you a blank canvas. We give you a finished painting. Pick a campaign, pick a month, click schedule — and it runs across email, social, AND a landing page. No writing, no designing, no posting."

### 2. "We already have Solutionreach / Demandforce"
"Solutionreach and Demandforce are great for automated reminders and recalls. But when was the last time they ran a 'Spring Allergies' campaign with matching Facebook posts, Instagram content, GBP updates, and a landing page? We're not replacing your recall system — we're filling the marketing gap that automated tools don't touch."

### 3. "We don't have time for marketing"
"That's exactly the point. You don't have time to plan a content calendar, design emails, write social posts, and build landing pages. That's why everything is pre-built. The entire marketing effort takes one click — literally. Your team doesn't create anything; they just approve the schedule."

### 4. "Email marketing doesn't work anymore"
"In healthcare it does — 21% open rates, which is higher than almost any other industry. And we're not just email. Each campaign runs across 5 channels, so even if a patient skips the email, they see the Facebook post, the Instagram story, or the GBP update. Multi-channel catches patients wherever they are."

### 5. "We tried campaigns before and stopped"
"Every practice that 'tried and stopped' ran into the same wall: the blank canvas. They ran out of ideas, time, or energy. Campaigns-To-Go removes that wall completely. The content exists. The design exists. The schedule exists. You just pick."

### 6. "How do I know it actually drives appointments?"
"We track it. The dashboard shows appointments booked within 7 days of email events — direct attribution from campaign send to exam chair. Across the network, 34,623 appointments were attributed to campaigns in a single month. That's not theoretical ROI — that's real patients in real chairs."

### 7. "My patients don't open marketing emails"
"The network average is 21% unique open rate — which is actually strong for email marketing. But more importantly, campaigns aren't just email. Each one also fires to Facebook, Instagram, and Google Business Profile. Even patients who skip the email see the campaign in their social feed."
