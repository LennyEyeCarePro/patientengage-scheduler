# Video Script: Campaigns-To-Go

## Format
- **Length:** 60–90 seconds
- **Style:** Screen recording walkthrough with voiceover
- **Audience:** Practice owners and office managers considering PatientEngage

---

## Script

### Opening (0:00–0:10)
**[Show: Office manager staring at blank Mailchimp editor, then closing the laptop]**

**Voiceover:** "You know you should be marketing your practice. But planning campaigns, designing emails, writing social posts, building landing pages — who has the time?"

### Problem (0:10–0:20)
**[Show: Empty Facebook page with last post 3 months ago, dusty content calendar]**

**Voiceover:** "Most practices try. They sign up for an email tool, post a few times, and then... silence. The content calendar goes blank. Patients stop hearing from you. And when they need an eye exam, they go wherever Google sends them."

### Solution Intro (0:20–0:35)
**[Show: Campaigns-To-Go library — scrolling through campaign options: Dry Eye, Spring Allergies, UV Protection, Save Vision Month]**

**Voiceover:** "Campaigns-To-Go changes that. Browse a library of hundreds of professionally designed campaigns — all built specifically for eye care. Pick one, pick a month, click schedule. That's it."

### Multi-Channel (0:35–0:50)
**[Show: Campaign detail — the same campaign pushing to Email, Facebook, Instagram, GBP, and Landing Page]**

**Voiceover:** "One click fires your campaign across five channels: email to your patient list, Facebook and Instagram posts, a Google Business Profile update, and a dedicated landing page. That's the kind of coordinated marketing agencies charge thousands for."

### How It Works (0:50–1:05)
**[Show: Campaign calendar — Live Campaigns and Future Campaigns with email timeline per location]**

**Voiceover:** "Each campaign runs on your schedule. Your locations get individual timelines — emails fire on the right dates, social posts publish automatically, and your landing page goes live. Multi-location? Every office stays in sync."

### Proof (1:05–1:20)
**[Show: Campaigns Report — Performance card with 3.7M emails, 775K opens, 34,623 appointments]**

**Voiceover:** "Across 647 practices, 3.7 million emails went out last month — with 34,000 appointments directly attributed to campaigns. That's not theoretical marketing. That's patients in chairs."

### Close (1:20–1:30)
**[Show: PatientEngage logo + CTA]**

**Voiceover:** "Campaigns-To-Go. Professional marketing at the touch of a button. No blank canvas. No content calendar. Just results. Ask your EyeCarePro team to get started today."
