# Page Brief: Campaigns-To-Go Marketing Page

## Page Goal
Convince optometry practice owners and office managers that Campaigns-To-Go eliminates the #1 barrier to practice marketing — the time, expertise, and effort required to create and execute professional multi-channel campaigns — by giving them a library of hundreds of pre-built, eye care-specific campaigns they can launch across 5 channels with one click.

## Target Audience
- Practice owners (ODs) who know they should be marketing but don't have time to plan, design, or execute
- Office managers who've been told to "post more on social media" and "send emails to patients" but have no content
- Multi-location group operators who need consistent marketing across all offices without hiring a per-location marketing coordinator
- Practices already paying for Mailchimp or Constant Contact but sending 0–2 emails/month because of the blank canvas problem
- Practices already using EyeCarePro for websites/SEO who haven't activated Campaigns-To-Go

## Key Messages (Priority Order)
1. **Marketing without a marketing department** — hundreds of campaigns pre-built and ready to go
2. **5 channels, 1 click** — Email + Facebook + Instagram + GBP + Landing Page, all at once
3. **Eye care-specific content** — not generic templates; real campaigns for real conditions and seasons
4. **Proof it drives appointments** — 34,623 campaign-attributed appointments in a single month
5. **3.7 million emails/month** — the infrastructure works at scale
6. **No blank canvas** — everything is pre-designed, pre-written, and pre-scheduled
