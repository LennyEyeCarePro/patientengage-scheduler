# Benefits: Campaigns-To-Go

## Primary Benefits

### 1. Marketing Without a Marketing Department
**Pain:** Practice owners know they should be marketing — sending emails, posting to social media, running seasonal promotions — but they don't have the time, the design skills, or the strategic knowledge. They either do nothing or pay $2,000–$5,000/month for an agency.
**Solution:** Campaigns-To-Go provides a library of hundreds of pre-built, professionally designed campaigns. Pick one, pick a month, click schedule. Done. No copywriting, no graphic design, no content calendars.
**Proof:** 647 practices running 741 campaigns in March 2026 — each one launched without the practice writing a word of copy or designing a single image.

### 2. Multi-Channel Reach in One Click
**Pain:** Running a proper marketing campaign means posting to Facebook, Instagram, Google Business Profile, sending an email blast, and creating a landing page. That's 5 separate platforms, 5 logins, 5 different content formats. Most practices do one channel (maybe email) and skip the rest.
**Solution:** Each Campaigns-To-Go campaign pushes content across all 5 channels simultaneously — Email, Facebook, Instagram, GBP, and a dedicated landing page. One click = full multi-channel execution.
**Proof:** Real campaigns show social post counts alongside email metrics — practices like 20/20 EyeCare Nacogdoches ran "Spring is Here, So Are Allergies" with 147 social posts plus 4,326 emails in a single campaign.

### 3. Real Data Connecting Marketing to Appointments
**Pain:** Most practices have no idea if their marketing actually works. They send emails and hope. They post to Facebook and never check if it generated a single appointment.
**Solution:** The campaign dashboard tracks the full funnel: Emails Sent → Opens → Clicks → Landing Page Views → Appointments booked within 7 days of email events. You can see exactly which campaigns drive patients into the chair.
**Proof:** 34,623 EHR email appointments tracked in March 2026 — direct attribution from campaign email to booked exam.

### 4. Eye Care-Specific Content Library
**Pain:** Generic email tools (Mailchimp, Constant Contact) give you a blank canvas. The practice still has to come up with the content, the subject lines, the images, the landing page. That's where most practices stall.
**Solution:** Every campaign in the library is designed specifically for eye care: Dry Eye tips, Spring Allergies, UV Protection, Save Vision Month, Women's Eye Health, Tax Season Eyewear, Diabetic Eyecare, brand-specific campaigns for Marchon, VSP, and Alcon. The content is ready. The images are ready. The landing pages are built.
**Proof:** Real campaign names from the network: "Tips to Relieve Dry Eye," "Spring is Here, So Are Allergies," "Eye Safety Awareness Workplace," "Save Vision Month Exam," "VSP April Sports Eye Safety" — all pre-built.

### 5. Scale Across Multiple Locations
**Pain:** Multi-location practices need consistent marketing across all offices. Running separate campaigns for each location is a logistical nightmare — different patient lists, different social accounts, different schedules.
**Solution:** Campaigns-To-Go lets you assign a single campaign to multiple locations. Each location gets its own email send, its own social posts, its own timeline — all managed from one view.
**Proof:** The Campaigns Manager shows per-location execution with individual send dates and status tracking across each location in a multi-location group.

### 6. Managed Scheduling and Oversight
**Pain:** Even with pre-built campaigns, practices forget to schedule, miss deadlines, or make mistakes. There's no accountability.
**Solution:** EyeCarePro's team manages campaign scheduling for many practices through the Campaigns Manager — monitoring queued emails, processing status, approval needs, and delay forecasts. The admin sees every practice's campaign status at a glance.
**Proof:** The Campaigns Manager Alerts card tracks: No Campaign, Emails Queued, Emails Processing, Emails Not Sent, Needs Approval — ensuring nothing falls through the cracks.

## Secondary Benefits
- **Brand partnership campaigns:** Marchon, VSP, Alcon campaigns let practices leverage manufacturer marketing dollars and co-branded content
- **Deliverability at scale:** 3.7M emails/month with only 1 practice exceeding 5% bounce rate — infrastructure is proven
- **Low complaint rate:** Only 4 practices exceeding 0.1% complaint rate — patients don't view campaign emails as spam
- **Seasonal automation:** Campaigns are pre-timed to awareness months (Save Vision Month in March, Women's Eye Health in April, UV Protection in summer) so practices are always relevant
- **Landing page ecosystem:** Each campaign generates a dedicated landing page, adding SEO-friendly content to the practice's web presence
