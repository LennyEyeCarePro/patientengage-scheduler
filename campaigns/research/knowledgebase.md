## Product Positioning
**Product Name:** Campaigns-To-Go
**Category:** Marketing (alongside Social Posts, Google Profile, Google Reviews in PE nav)
**Tagline:** Marketing at the Touch of a Button
**Core Purpose:** Give practices professional, multi-channel marketing campaigns without requiring them to plan, design, write, or schedule anything.

### Key Messaging
- A library of hundreds of pre-built eye care campaigns ready to schedule with one click
- Multi-channel: Email + Facebook + Instagram + Google Business Profile + Landing Pages
- Professionally designed content — no blank canvas, no DIY
- Real-time performance tracking with appointment attribution
- Managed by EyeCarePro's team — no practice staff time required
- Eye care-specific: seasonal awareness campaigns, brand partnerships, clinical education
- Scales across multi-location practices — assign campaigns per location

## How It Works
1. **Browse:** Practice (or ECP team) browses the campaign library — organized by topic, season, brand partner
2. **Select:** Choose a campaign — e.g., "Spring is Here, So Are Allergies" — and assign it to one or more locations
3. **Schedule:** Pick a start/end date (monthly slots). The campaign goes into the schedule.
4. **Execute:** On the scheduled date, the system sends emails to the practice's patient list, posts to connected social channels (FB, IG, GBP), and publishes the campaign landing page
5. **Track:** Performance data flows into the dashboard — Emails Sent, Opens, Clicks, Page Views, and EHR Email Appointments (appointments booked within 7 days of email activity)
6. **Manage:** EyeCarePro's admin team monitors all practices through the Campaigns Manager, handling approvals, delays, and troubleshooting

### Important Distinctions
- **Not DIY** — practices don't create campaigns; they choose from a pre-built library
- **Not just email** — each campaign spans up to 5 channels
- **Not automated recalls** — campaigns are marketing content, not appointment reminders (that's Recalls & Reminders)
- **Not patient-specific** — campaigns go to the entire patient list or uploaded segments, not individual patients
- **Admin-managed** — many practices are scheduled by EyeCarePro's team, not self-service

## What Makes Campaigns-To-Go Different

1. **Done-for-you, not DIY:** Pre-built campaigns with professional design, copy, images, and landing pages — not a blank email editor
2. **5-channel coordination:** Email + FB + IG + GBP + Landing Page in one click — no other optometry platform does this
3. **Eye care content library:** Hundreds of campaigns designed for optometry — not generic health templates rebranded
4. **Brand partnership ecosystem:** Marchon, VSP, Alcon campaigns with co-branded content — leveraging manufacturer marketing dollars
5. **Appointment attribution:** EHR Email Appointments connect campaign emails to actual booked exams — real ROI proof
6. **Scale:** 3.7M emails/month across 647 practices with near-zero bounce and complaint rates — proven infrastructure
7. **Managed service:** EyeCarePro's team handles scheduling, approvals, and troubleshooting — practices don't have to self-serve
8. **Multi-location support:** Assign campaigns per-location with individual timelines — no duplicating effort
9. **Complements the stack:** Works alongside RingAnalytics (calls), 2-Way Texting (conversations), Recalls & Reminders (patient outreach), and the Real Time Scheduler (online booking) as part of the PatientEngage marketing suite

## Best Use Case
Use Campaigns-To-Go when you want professional, multi-channel marketing campaigns without the time, cost, or expertise to do it yourself. For patient-specific outreach (recalls, reminders, confirmations), use Recalls & Reminders. For real-time conversations, use 2-Way Texting. For call tracking and attribution, use RingAnalytics.
