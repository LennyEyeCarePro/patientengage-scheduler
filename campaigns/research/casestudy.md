# Case Study: Campaigns-To-Go — Coastal Eye Associates

## Practice Profile
- **Anonymized Name:** Coastal Eye Associates
- **Real Practice:** Maine Optometry
- **State:** Maine (New England)
- **Practice Type:** Full-service optometry practice with large patient base
- **Data Period:** March 2026
- **Campaign:** Dry Eye Rescue

## The Challenge
Coastal Eye Associates is a well-established optometry practice in New England with a large, loyal patient base. Despite strong clinical reputation and thousands of patients on file, the practice had no consistent way to reach patients between visits. Email marketing tools like Mailchimp sat dormant — the office manager opened the editor once, stared at the blank screen, and never sent a campaign. Meanwhile, Dry Eye was the practice's fastest-growing service line, and patients who could benefit from treatment simply didn't know it was available.

## The Numbers (March 2026)

### Campaign: Dry Eye Rescue
A single Campaigns-To-Go campaign — "Dry Eye Rescue" — targeting the full patient list with educational content about dry eye symptoms, treatment options, and a call-to-action to schedule an evaluation.

### Email Performance
| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Emails Sent | 132,220 |
| Total Opens | 52,969 |
| Unique Opens | 42,250 (32%) |
| Email Clicks | 1,150 (0.9%) |
| Bounces | 632 (0.5%) |
| Complaints | 11 (0.0%) |
| Page Views | 0 (0%) |
| EHR Email Appointments | 1,468 (1.1%) |

### Key Performance Highlights
- **32% unique open rate** — well above the 21% network average. Patients trust this practice and open their emails.
- **1,468 booked appointments** directly attributed to the campaign (appointments within 7 days of email engagement)
- **0.5% bounce rate** — clean email list, strong deliverability
- **0.0% complaint rate** — patients want to hear from this practice
- **1.1% appointment conversion** — above the 0.9% network average

## Revenue Math

### Campaign-Attributed Appointments
- 1,468 appointments booked within 7 days of email engagement
- At $350/exam average: **$513,800 in campaign-influenced revenue from a single campaign**
- Dry Eye evaluations often lead to ongoing treatment plans (IPL, punctal plugs, prescription drops) worth $500–$2,000+ per patient over 12 months
- **Potential lifetime value from one campaign: $1.5M–$3M+**

### Marketing Cost Comparison
- Equivalent agency work (large-scale email campaign + creative + list management): $3,000–$8,000/month
- Campaigns-To-Go: Included in EyeCarePro marketing program
- **ROI: $513K revenue from $0 incremental marketing spend**

## What This Campaign Revealed

1. **Volume proves the system works at scale** — 132,220 emails delivered with only 0.5% bounces and 0.0% complaints. The infrastructure handles massive lists without deliverability problems.

2. **Open rates reward relevance** — 32% unique open rate vs. 21% network average. When the campaign topic matches a real patient need (dry eye), engagement skyrockets. Pre-built Campaigns-To-Go content is designed for exactly this kind of relevance.

3. **One campaign, 1,468 appointments** — direct attribution from email engagement to booked exams. No guessing if marketing works. The dashboard shows exactly which patients opened the email and booked within 7 days.

4. **Dry Eye is a revenue multiplier** — the initial exam is just the beginning. Patients diagnosed through this campaign enter treatment plans worth 3–5x the initial visit. One campaign seeds an entire revenue stream.

5. **Zero effort from the practice** — the practice didn't write the email, design the creative, build the landing page, or manage the send. They selected "Dry Eye Rescue" from the library, scheduled it, and the system handled everything.
