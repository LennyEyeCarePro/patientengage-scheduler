## Product Positioning (from EyeCarePro.com)
**Headline:** "Answer the Call with RingAnalytics"
**Subhead:** "Do You Know How Your Phones Are Being Answered?"
**Core Hook:** "Are your phones ringing off the hook but your calendar's still blank?"

### Key Messaging from Product Page
- Positions the phone as the #1 patient touchpoint that most practices don't measure
- Leads with the pain: patients stop booking and start looking for another practice — and the practice never knows why
- AI platform that lets you "hear and see for yourself" how many calls converted and WHY they didn't
- HIPAA-compliant: every call routed through the platform, recorded, transcribed, analyzed
- Cites Healthgrades/Stax stat: 85% of patients still book appointments over the phone
- Double Optimizer Bundle pitch: offices offering both phone and online scheduling saw 24% increase in phone calls

### Feature List (from Product Page)
1. Playback or read transcript of any call on the dashboard
2. Track missed calls and calls that didn't result in bookings
3. Identify customer experience missteps or staff training opportunities
4. Strategize ways to increase appointment bookings
5. Deep dive to see why one location may outperform another
6. Track call volume throughout the day for optimal phone staffing
7. Compare month-over-month appointment bookings and patient calls

### Value Propositions (from Product Page)
- Get insights into staff performance
- Compare performance across all practice locations
- Optimize office resources
- Miss fewer calls
- Book more appointments

## Admin Platform Deep-Dive

### Network Overview (Q1 2026: Jan 1 – Mar 30)
- 189 practices, 484 locations
- 324,348 total calls tracked (↑50% YoY)
- 83,717 appointment calls (26% of all calls, ↑68% YoY)
- 58,418 booked calls (70% of appointment calls, ↑71% YoY)
- Average call length: 2:48
- 3,810 new patients identified
- 9,251 returning patients identified

### Admin Report Columns
The admin Calls report shows per-location data across these metrics:
- Location Name, City
- Total Calls (with YoY % change)
- Appt Calls (count, % of total, YoY change)
- Booked (count, % of total, YoY change)
- New Patients (count, YoY change)
- Returning Patients (count, YoY change)
- Capture % (booked ÷ appt calls, YoY change)
- Service calls (count, % of total, YoY change)
- Missed calls (count, % of total, YoY change)
- Spam (count, % of total)
- Avg Duration (mm:ss, YoY change)

### Alert Thresholds
- Call Drop >25%: 14 practices flagged (↓22% — improving)
- Appt Calls <35%: 235 practices flagged
- Appt Capture <60%: 75 practices flagged
- Missed Calls >15%: 207 practices flagged

### Practice Detail View Features
When clicking into a specific practice, the dashboard shows:
1. **Summary Cards:** Total Calls, Average Duration, Appointment Calls, Missed Calls, Booked Calls, Capture Rate — each with YoY comparison arrows
2. **Calls by Type Donut Chart:** Booked, Not Booked, Hang Up, No Answer, Busy, Voicemail, Spam, Service — with percentages
3. **Call Volume Trends:** Month-over-month line chart (current month vs. last month vs. two months ago)
4. **All Calls Heatmap:** Day-of-week × Hour-of-day matrix showing call density
5. **Call Log Table:** Every individual call with Date/Time, Duration, Call Type, Location, Caller #, Office #, Source, Medium, and AI-generated keyword Tags
6. **Source/Medium Filters:** Filter calls by source (OnSite, OffSite) and medium (GMB, Google Ads, Other Directories)
7. **Search:** Full-text search across call data
8. **CSV Export:** Download button for all call data

### AI-Generated Tags (observed in call logs)
Tags automatically applied by AI based on call transcript analysis:
- Contact Lenses
- Glasses And Contacts Issue
- Insurance Issue (BI BI BI Insurance, Health Insurance, V S P Insurance)
- Prescription
- Location / Directions Issue
- Time Issue
- Annual (annual exam)
- Email

### Source/Medium Attribution
- **OnSite:** Calls from the practice's own website
- **OffSite:** Calls from external directories and listings
- **Mediums:** GMB (Google Business Profile), Google Ads, Other Directories (Healthgrades, Yelp, Zocdoc, etc.)

## What Makes RingAnalytics Different from Generic Call Tracking

1. **Optometry-trained AI:** Scoring model trained on data from 189 optometry practices — understands exam types, insurance questions, optical orders, contact lens follow-ups
2. **Booking classification:** Every call scored as Booked/Not Booked, not just "answered/missed"
3. **Patient type detection:** AI identifies New Patient vs. Returning Patient calls
4. **Marketing attribution built in:** Source + Medium tracking connects phone calls to marketing channels (GMB, Google Ads, directories)
5. **Multi-location comparison:** Admin view lets corporate/multi-location practices compare performance across all locations
6. **HIPAA-compliant:** Full call recording with encrypted storage — not an afterthought
7. **Keyword tagging:** AI extracts topics discussed (insurance, contact lenses, glasses, prescriptions) for pattern analysis
8. **Staffing intelligence:** Heatmap shows exact hours when call volume peaks, enabling optimal phone staffing
